Our Services

One Off Kleen

Whether you're hosting a party, preparing for guests, or just need a one-time clean, our One Off Kleen service has got you covered. We'll provide a detailed clean of your home or office to ensure everything is spotless for your special occasion.

Sparkle Kleen

Our Sparkle Kleen service is designed to provide a thorough and efficient clean to make your home or office shine. Our experienced cleaners will work diligently to leave your space looking and feeling fresh and clean.

Deep Kleen

Our Deep Kleen service is perfect for those who want a more comprehensive and intensive clean. We'll go beyond the regular cleaning tasks to provide a deep clean that will leave your space feeling revitalized and refreshed. Whether it's for your home or office, we'll take care of all the cleaning details so you can enjoy a pristine environment.

COT Kleen

Moving into or out of a home or office can be a busy and stressful time. Our Change Of Tenancy Klean service is designed to provide a comprehensive clean of your old or new space, ensuring that it's ready for the next tenant. We'll deep clean every room, including bathrooms and kitchens, to leave your old space spotless or your new space fresh and clean. Our experienced cleaners will work efficiently to get the job done quickly and effectively, leaving you with one less thing to worry about during this busy time.

Office Kleen

A clean and tidy office can make all the difference in productivity and employee satisfaction. Our Office Kleen service is designed to provide a thorough clean of your office space, including desk cleaning, bathroom cleaning, and floor maintenance, to ensure a clean and comfortable workspace for everyone.

ABC Kleen

Building or renovating can leave a lot of mess behind, but our After Build Kleen service is here to help. Our team will come in and clean up all the dust, debris, and construction materials to leave your space clean and ready to use.
